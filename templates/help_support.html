Frequently Asked Questions

To create a new shift handover, navigate to the "Handover" page from the sidebar menu, fill in the required information including incidents, key points, and next shift details, then click "Submit Handover".

Go to the "Reports" section in the sidebar menu. You can filter reports by date, shift type, and status. Click on any report to view detailed information.

Access the "Roster" page to view and manage shift schedules. You can add team members, assign shifts, and upload roster files in Excel format.

Click on your profile dropdown in the sidebar and select "My Profile". In the profile page, scroll down to the "Change Password" section and follow the instructions.

The application works best with modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. We recommend using the latest version for optimal performance.

In the Reports section, use the export buttons to download reports in various formats including PDF, Excel, and CSV. You can also customize the export settings.
